Volkswagen
(VWAGY)

Volkswagen (VWAGY) is a Germany-based multinational automotive manufacturer. It develops and produces passenger cars, trucks, and light commercial vehicles. Models include the Tiguan, Golf, Jetta, and Passat. The company stopped making the Volkswagen Beetle in 2019. Luxury brands include Porsche and Audi.

Toyota (TM) is a Japan-based multinational company known for efficiency and quality. It designs and manufactures cars, trucks, and commercial vehicles, including the Corolla, Camry, 4Runner, Tacoma, and Prius. Lexus is its luxury division.
